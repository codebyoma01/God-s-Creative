Who We Are

Our Story

God's Creative Events was born from a deep passion for creating beautiful, meaningful experiences rooted in Nigerian culture. Based across Aba and Umuahia in Abia State, we have grown into one of Southeast Nigeria's most trusted event planning, decoration and catering companies. We have had the honour of serving weddings, traditional Igbo ceremonies, corporate functions, and birthday celebrations bringing the same dedication, creativity, and warmth to every single event. And when our clients need us further afield, we travel because no event is too far when excellence is the standard.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional event planning, decoration and catering rooted in authentic Nigerian culture combining professional excellence with warm, personal service that makes every event uniquely memorable for our clients and their guests.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted event planning and catering company in Nigeria known for authenticity, creativity, reliability, and the ability to bring any vision to life with elegance and grace.
